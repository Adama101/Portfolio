About Me

Background Info: FinTech

I am a results-driven professional with a keen focus on addressing challenges and delivering effective solutions, supported by a robust track record spanning more than five years in the FinTech sector. My expertise encompasses various roles in the Remittances and Payments industry in The Gambia, where I have played a pivotal role in advancing financial inclusion initiatives across Africa. As a collaborative team player, I bring strong leadership qualities and an unwavering commitment to continuous skill development. My methodical and strategic approach has consistently yielded outstanding results in project management. One of my notable achievements includes spearheading the establishment of Kuringo, the inaugural FinTech company in The Gambia. This venture, covering Remittances, Payments, Savings, and Loans, underscores my dedication to enhancing financial inclusion in the region. Beyond my professional pursuits, I am deeply passionate about technology and entrepreneurship, boasting over five years of experience in the Financial Technology landscape. My contributions to various Remittances companies in The Gambia have significantly contributed to the broader goal of fostering financial inclusion in Africa. As a tech entrepreneur, I am eager to introduce novel concepts to the African e-commerce space, leveraging innovative technologies to create a substantial impact.
